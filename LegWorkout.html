Leg Workouts

"Never skip leg day" is what we are told. This statement is very true, even thogh you may skip it alot. Legs are importaat to building up the body you want. Each of these workouts will work all the muscles in your legs and it will be all you need.

The Squat

Probably the most well known workout, the squat is brilliant because all of the muscles it works, and because of that its a great calorie burner. The squat can be weighted or unweighted. For this demonstration it will be weighted. This is an exercise that needs to be done properly as it has high risk of injury. Your feet need to be apart around shoulder with, Back must be strght at all times. 8 reps for 3 sets

The Wall Sit

Dont be fooled, the wall sit is a workout thats as tough as they come. It may be simple on paper, but in practice its very tough. Allyou need for this workout is of course a wall. Stand with you back to the wall and lower yourself until you legs are at a 90 degree angle. Do this for 30 seconds for 3 sets

The Step Up

the step up is an easy workout that works up a sweat. It requries more muscles to be used thus burning more caloies. to do this you need a bench or chair. Set 1 foot ont the bench, without jumping slowly lift youself with the power of the leg on the bench. Once up on the bench go back down and repeat. 8 each leg for 3 sets
